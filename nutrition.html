Why Nutrition Matters

Eating right is just as important as working out. Good nutrition helps you build muscle, burn fat, recover faster, and stay energized throughout the day.

Healthy Eating Basics

Healthy eating doesn’t mean eating perfectly — it means being consistent. Focus on:

  • Lean proteins: chicken, eggs, fish, tofu
  • Whole carbs: rice, potatoes, oats, pasta
  • Healthy fats: nuts, avocado, olive oil
  • Fruits & vegetables every day
  • Drinking enough water

How to Track Calories

Tracking calories is a simple way to understand your body:

  • Eat more than you burn → gain weight
  • Eat less than you burn → lose weight
  • Eat the same as you burn → maintain weight

Don’t aim for perfection — aim for consistency.
